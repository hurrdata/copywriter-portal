About Our Antioch, TN Location

Located just south of Nashville and conveniently located near the intersection of Antioch Pike and Haywood Ln (less than a mile west of J.E. Moss Elementary School via Richards Rd), our Antioch Pike location is perfectly located with easy interstate access to serve residents of Antioch and Nashville.

  • Conveniently located for Antioch (37013) residents
  • A convenient option for Nashville residents in the Nolensville Pike/Berry Hill corridor (37211) looking for an affordable self storage option.
  • Just 0.2 miles from I-24, Exit 57 (Haywood Lane) — easy on, easy off for every storage run
  • Minutes from established Antioch neighborhoods including Antioch Park, Oakwood Terrace, Barclay Square, and Bakertown
