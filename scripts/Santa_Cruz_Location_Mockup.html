About Our Santa Cruz, CA Location

Conveniently located near the intersection of Mission St and Fair Ave at 1310 Fair Ave, we serve the unique needs of Santa Cruz residents. Whether you're a Westside resident looking for more space or a UCSC student between semesters, we offer the convenience and flexibility you need.

  • Located in the heart of Westside in Santa Cruz (95060)
  • Easy access from Mission St (Hwy 1) makes this an ideal location for renters, students, and residents in Westside, Downtown, and even Seabright.
  • Less than 1 mile from UC Santa Cruz to the south, this facility offers great options for students including smaller, affordable units like 5×5 and 5×10.
  • Just minutes from residential areas like The Circles and apartments like Pacific Shores and The Hilltop.
